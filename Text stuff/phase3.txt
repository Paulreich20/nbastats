players.csv contains base NBA player data from 1985 to 2018 including, name, career start year, career end year(2018 if still active),
height, weight, univeristy, and position

season_stats,csv contains season statistics of each NBA player from 1985 to 2018 including,
points, team, assists, offensive rebounds, defensive rebounds, steals, blocks, free throw percentage, 3 point percentage, field goal percentage, minutes played, games played, personal fouls, and age. 

api-tests contains our various unit test which while not high in number test the various types of edge cases in our queries.
